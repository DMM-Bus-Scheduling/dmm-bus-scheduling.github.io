How It Works

Our optimization engine transforms complex transit data into efficient, electric vehicle schedules — minimizing fleet size while ensuring full coverage.

🔍 Data Inputs

  • 🧭 Stops & Routes: Map of all stop connections
  • 🚌 Trips: Arrival and departure times per trip
  • 🗓️ Schedules: Weekdays, weekends, and seasonal types
  • 🔋 Vehicles: Battery capacity, discharge & charge rates
  • 🏁 Depots: Parking and charging locations
  • 📏 Distances & Durations: Between all points

📈 Outputs You Get

  • ✅ Minimal fleet size needed to meet all trip demand
  • 🕒 Schedules for each vehicle across all time types
  • 🔌 Charging plans that respect energy limits
  • 🔄 Route switching logic between compatible trips

⚙️ Optimization Modes

⚡ Fast Mode: Graph-based and greedy algorithms for quick results.

🧠 Optimal Mode: Exact fleet minimization using linear programming.
